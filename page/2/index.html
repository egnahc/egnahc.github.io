关于自律与例行公事

听“幸福公开课”，慢慢的能听下去了。跟其他的成功学一样，内容都是大家熟知的，但不一样的，也是我愿意花时间学习的是，它强调的科学性，有理有据，而且顺从人的本性，不强求，不做作。

今天听到一个有意思的说法。大多数人都承认自律的人一般都能做成自己想做的事。这没错，但不是每个人都可以那么自律的。更糟糕的是，这个自律的能力基本没法增进。那怎么办呢？其实，自律这个东西并不那么重要，不影响你成功。我们应该把要做的事看成例行公事，也就是把任何你觉得重要的事情都放到日常的安排当中，剩下就是按部就班了。这个就是执行力的问题，把事情简单化，公式化，安排时间，然后执行。

这也是为什么我老婆做的比我好的地方。她工作或者学习的方式比较简单，安排好时间，一个任务一个任务的完成，而且从心底里当做是固定的行程。比方说，刚开始为了熟悉金融市场，那么每天读新闻写简报，2、3年过去了，从开始不知道读什么看什么，到现在如数家珍。这种积累的爆发就是例行公事的结果。反过来，这么多年来，我暑假都基本没有做过有意思的事情。读书的时候觉得暑假可以多看书增加知识，但没有这个执行力，没有当做日常来看待。一直纠结于自己的自律性差，好多次都无功而返。

当然，我们也得承认能实现这个“例行公事”也需要坚持。所以，让我们坚持简单的工作方式，聚沙成塔！

2016-09-01

Change

I am in the crossroad of my life.

I am a postdoc in mathematics, but could not find a tenure track position. It is even impossible since my wife has a good job now and would like to develop her career in the company for a long time. That being said, I have to find a position near where she works. We keep the long distance relationship for more than three years now.

I read a lot of articles complaining how cheap a postdoc is and how distressful the life is. I have no regret doing research in mathematics because I love it. People hold the dreams because they have passion for it. The same for me. However, if you don’t enjoy what you are doing, especially you cannot see any future of what you are doing, I don’t know why you still waste your time.

If you look at the mathematics world in recent 10 or 20 years, there is seldom exciting advancement. Yeah, you may say there are a couple of millennium problems solved, but so what? It is so obvious that the supply of mathematicians as researchers is way more than the demand in the job market. Most of time, when mathematics PhDs and postodcs meet together, people are very pessimistic about their future. This is the winter for mathematicians, and this is the reality now.

Well, some of us may think about taking a position in a teaching school. I am good at teaching, and I love teaching as well. If I find a position like that near my wife, I would definitely take it. Do you think teaching entry level mathematics has a bright future or do you think you can do this in your whole life? Look around! More and more universities and colleges start long distance or new technology education. Mathematics education can not avoid it. I personally prefer face to face class meeting. But if there is a choice that students can watch a video and finish homework online, like most computer science classes, especially the interaction part serves them well, I don’t really believe I can beat the video class. Here are two important components for the future of mathematics education. The first is all the entry level courses can be taught by online classes, even by a machine. The second is for senior and famous researcher. This kind of change of work distribution will happen to other vocation too.

After evaluating my capability and reality, I know the chance I become a senior (permanent) researcher is slim. If I could teach behind a camera, I am OK with it. But still you need to learn more technology to keep up with the development. The most important one is to learn how to communicate with machines. For example, how to teach a class of 10,000 students at a time, how can you deal with the discussion session with assistant of machines, how can you tell machines to grade the homework in the way you like, etc.?

My point here is that the future of the world is machine. People, who can communicate and cooperate with machine can stand out.

So I need a change! I want to become a machine expert in a more theoretical way so that I can apply what I learned in mathematics to the future job.
